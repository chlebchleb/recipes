Oven Pan 'Pizza'

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Grated Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Green Pepper
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to whatever degrees Celcius idk
  2. Cover pan in parchment paper
  3. Lay down slices of white bread until bottom of pan is filled
  4. Spread Tomato Sauce on bread, and then three quarters of the Cheese on top of the Tomato Sauce
  5. Add literally everything else (evenly) and then add the rest of the Cheese on top
  6. Bake until it's cooked but not burnt
