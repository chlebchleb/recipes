Black Tea

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Black Tea
  • Fresh Ginger
  • Cinnamon Stick

Steps:

  1. Boil water equal to 4/5ths of the desired cup size.
  2. Add 1 Packet of Black Tea, 1 stick of Cinnamon, 3-4 slices of Ginger, Sugar to taste.
  3. Pour the water in the cup, mix, let rest for 2 minutes.
  4. Add Milk until cup is almost full, mix again.
  5. Drink when it doesn't burn your tongue lmfao
