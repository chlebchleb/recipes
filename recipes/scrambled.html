Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Grated Cheese
  • Salt, Pepper

Steps

  1. Fry bacon on low temp for a few mins
  2. While bacon is frying, dice onion
  3. Add onion in with the bacon
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs, add salt and pepper
  5. Pour eggs into pan, stir constantly
  6. While egg still runny, add cheese, continue stirring
  7. Once cheese melted and egg solid consistency, kill heat and serve
  8. Let cool for few mins, optionally add oregano or some other herbs idk
